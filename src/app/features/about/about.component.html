My Story

I've spent the past 6 years turning ideas into fast, beautiful, and scalable web applications. What began as simple curiosity became an obsession, crafting experiences that not only work flawlessly but feel amazing to use.

I've mastered the art of frontend development, coding in Angular and React with equal fluency, and bridging the gap to the backend with Java and Node.js. Across enterprise platforms serving millions and dynamic startups, I've built, led, and shipped projects that deliver real impact. After moving to Asia, I built my own full-stack application from scratch, designing, coding, and deploying every part myself, which deepened my love for crafting high-performance, elegant interfaces.

Clean architecture, performance, and pixel-perfect design aren't just goals for me, they're my standards. I take pride in writing elegant, maintainable code that teams love to build upon.

Recently, I made the biggest move of my life, to Asia, to start a new chapter and build an exciting, long-term career. I'm here to create, to lead, and to bring world-class frontend engineering to the region's most ambitious products.

Because for me, this isn't just code, it's craft.
